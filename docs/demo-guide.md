Suggested QA journeys:

- Open a public traveler plan and inspect creator + member cards, then open the linked traveler profiles.
- Open an agency profile and inspect packages plus recent reviews.
- Log in as a traveler and review the offer inbox for comparison, negotiation, and referred offers.
- Log in as an agency owner and open the referral inbox to inspect traveler-referred plans.
- Open `/dashboard/messages` and verify seeded traveler-to-traveler conversations.
- Open confirmed and completed trip chats to verify seeded system messages, traveler messages, and polls.
- Open confirmed and completed plans to inspect payment state, committed member status, and review surfaces.
